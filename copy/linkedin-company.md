## About (up to 2,000 characters)

[COMPANY_NAME] is a B2B creator sourcing platform for brands that need targeted audiences — not just large ones.

We find and package content creators in the 1K–20K range, matched to a brand's specific demographic brief. Automated discovery. Vetted shortlists. Commission-based deals.

**The gap we're filling:**
Creator ad spend is $37B and growing 4× faster than total media. But every major agency sets a minimum audience threshold — 100K, 50K, or higher. Below that, the tier is unrepresented. Not because the audiences aren't valuable. Because finding, vetting, and contracting small creators at scale has always required more manual work than the economics justified.

We built the infrastructure to do it programmatically. That's the moat.

**How it works:**
Brands brief us on their target audience — demographics, geography, niche. Our engine surfaces ranked creator matches, scored on engagement quality, audience location, commercial history, and content fit. We present a vetted bundle. The brand approves. We handle outreach, negotiation, and contracts.

Commission-based. No retainer. We get paid when deals close.

**Who it's for:**
Marketing managers, CMOs, and growth leads at brands that advertise in niche verticals — fintech, healthtech, devtools, wellness, consumer — who want creator placements that actually reach their audience.
