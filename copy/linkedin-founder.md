Most creator advertising is built for big audiences.

Agencies set six-figure-follower minimums. Platforms optimize for reach. The tier where niche precision is highest — engaged, specific, smaller — gets skipped. Not because it doesn't work. Because finding and vetting creators below the agency floor doesn't scale manually.

I started CastWorks to close that gap.

Right now we're focused on men's lifestyle DTC — wallets, bags, knives, watches, grooming, kit subscriptions. Brands in the $5–50M revenue band, already running creator sponsorships or actively scaling CAC channels.
