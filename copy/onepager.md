## How it works

2. **Match** — Our engine scores creators against your brief: engagement quality, audience location, commercial history, content fit.
3. **Review** — We present a vetted shortlist with per-creator rationale. You approve the bundle.
4. **Close** — We handle outreach, negotiation, and contracts. You see results.
